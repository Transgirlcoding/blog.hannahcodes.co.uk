blog 4- my trip to see Everyone's talking about jamie.

29th june, 2022 posted by hannah

On the 4th jan of this year (2022) i went to see everybody's talking bout Jamie at the Mayflower theatre in Southamption with my LGBTQA+ support group, as they were able to geet some tickets to see the show

Everybody's talking about Jamie is about a 16 year old boy who wants to be a professional drag queen when he is older, at first he gets made fun off but in the end with help from famous Drag queen Loco Chanel, he becomes the most famous Drag queen and becomes much stronger to fightback the haters.

After watching with some of my lgbtqa+ friends and support group adults, i would like to say it was amazing, one of the best theatre perfomnces i've seen in a while and with it been 2 years since a performnce like that can take place.

if you're into musicals and drag queens/ LGBTQA+ shows I hightly recommend you go and watch when you have the chance. I even picked up some merch too

Everyone is talking about jamie. LGBTQA+/Drag queen
